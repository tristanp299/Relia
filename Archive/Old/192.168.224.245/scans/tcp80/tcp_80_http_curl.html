Free 24/7 Support

The RELIA 24/7 hotline is a free service that provides timber specialists with around-the-clock access to expert advice and support. The hotline is staffed by experienced professionals who can provide advice and support on a wide range of topics, including timber selection, treatment and preservation, and construction methods. The hotline is available to all timber specialists, regardless of experience or qualifications.

Our Story

The company RELIA was founded in Europe in the early 1990s. The company specializes in technologies for the lumber industry, including sawmills, wood-processing plants, and furniture factories. RELIA's products are used in more than 40 countries around the world..
